# Technical Summary: CADAF S.A. Digital Environment & Growth (2026)

**Source**: Multi-platform search (News, Official Site, Export Directories, Industry Reports).
**Date**: 2026-04-11
**Subject**: Expansion and technological requirements for Alfalfa export in San Francisco, Córdoba.

## Key Technical Findings
- **Production Hub**: San Francisco, Córdoba (Strategic point due to Belgrano Cargas rail link).
- **Core Technology**: 
    - Custom-engineered compression and drying systems. 
    - High-density megafardos (standard for Middle East premium markets).
- **Supply Chain**: 
    - Fixed-price annual contracts with local producers. 
    - Managed via traditional logistics/industrial protocols. 
- **Critical Infrastructure Updates (Q1 2026)**:
    - Inauguration of expanded storage units (Galpones) for finished goods.
    - Saturation of existing manual/analog management processes due to volume doubling.

## Identifided Pain Points (AgTech Opportunities)
1. **Saturation of Quality Control**: The volume of producers and megafardos makes manual moisture/protein tracking a bottleneck for export containers.
2. **Logistical Visibility**: Tensions in the Red Sea (Strait of Hormuz) create a need for real-time tracking of containers to manage buyer expectations in Saudi Arabia/UAE.
3. **Contract Digitization**: Moving from phone/spreadsheet contracts to a digital framework that includes automated quality triggers.

## Market Positioning
CADAF is the prime target for an **Alfalfa Traceability SaaS** (Java/Quarkus) that integrates field sensors with port logistics, ensuring the "Digital Quality Certificate" required by Saudi buyers.
